Civic Innovation Labs

Long-Term Collaboration

Civic Innovation Labs (CILs) are structured, volunteer-driven spaces where professionals work alongside government partners to design and implement technology, data, and policy solutions that improve public services.

Hackathons

Innovate and Build Fast

Hackathons bring together developers, designers, and civic leaders to develop open-source solutions for real world challenges. These fast-paced events encourage creative problem-solving and rapid prototyping.

Guftugu

Shaping the Future Through Civic Dialogue

Guftugu is a space for deep, open ended civic discussions where complex challenges like climate, water, governance, and social equity are explored through the lens of civic technology. This is where ideas take shape, where experts, activists, and technologists debate solutions, and where conversations spark action.
